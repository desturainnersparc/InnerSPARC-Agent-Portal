  • Certificate remains centered and proportional in each viewport panel.
  • No vertical clipping and no excessive empty space below certificate.
  • Actions and footer remain inside one panel container at every breakpoint.
  • Only Prev and Download buttons are visible in the footer.
  • No fullscreen or share controls are present.
  • Download and Prev buttons remain clickable in all breakpoints.
  • Downloaded PDF/PNG is high-resolution (300+ DPI) suitable for print.
  • Export size matches certificate pixel bounds exactly with no extra whitespace.
