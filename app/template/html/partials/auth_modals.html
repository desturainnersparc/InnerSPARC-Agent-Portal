Our Journey

Established in 2019


Inner SPARC Realty Corporation began as a small dream and quickly grew into a thriving business, thanks to the trust of our valued clients. Founded on October 14, 2019, by Licensed Real Estate Broker Gabriel Villamor Libacao Jr., REB, and Mr. Erwin G. Baguioan, Inner SPARC transitioned from a humble sole proprietorship into a corporation driven by a passion for helping Filipinos achieve homeownership.

Our journey is about more than growth - it's about commitment. We understand the importance of buying a home, and we're dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality properties that meet the needs of Filipino families. Fully compliant with Department of Trade and Industry regulations, Inner SPARC is committed to transparency and ethics, making homeownership a reality for everyone.

Gabriel V. Libacao Jr.
CEO/Founder

Vision

At Inner Sparc Realty Corporation, we envision a future where every Filipino family finds their dream home with ease and confidence. We are committed to transforming the real estate experience by making it accessible, affordable, and personalized, ensuring that every homebuyer and investor feels valued and supported throughout their journey.

Mission

Our mission is to empower Filipino families and individuals by providing exceptional real estate services that cater to their unique needs and aspirations. We strive to deliver innovative solutions, unparalleled customer service, and a deep understanding of local communities. Through our dedication to integrity and excellence, we aim to build lasting relationships and contribute to the fulfillment of homeownership dreams across the Philippines.
